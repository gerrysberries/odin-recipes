Korean Fried Chicken

Description

This recipe will change your life - sweet & spicy chicken with the perfect amount of crisp!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup potato starch
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

For the sauce:

  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup rice syrup
  • 1/4 cup gochujang
  • 2 tsp white vinegar
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • corn (or vegetable oil) for frying
